The cities you choose will display basic regional details such as temperature, weather conditions, humidity, wind speed, and time zone.

PM2.5 air quality refers to the amount of fine particulate matter (PM), which consists of microscopic particles less than 2.5 micrometers in diameter, in the air. These tiny, inhalable particles, including smoke, dust, and aerosols, are the most dangerous type of PM because they can penetrate deep into the lungs and may even enter the bloodstream.
